#### Business segments

* Computing and Graphics segment primarily includes desktop and notebook processors, discrete and integrated GPUs, data center and professional GPUs, and development service.

* Enterprise, Embedded and Semi-Custom segment includes server and embedded processors, technology for game consoles, and semi-custom system-on-chips (SoCs).

Examples of the company’s semi-custom SoC products can be found in PlayStation and Xbox games consoles, with AMD processors and graphics cards to feature in the PS5 and Xbox Series X consoles that are set to be released in November 2020.
